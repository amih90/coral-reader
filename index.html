Coral Reader

Read beautifully. Anywhere. A modern, distraction-free reader for EPUB & PDF, built for Android and iOS.

📱 Android iOS 📖 EPUB & PDF

One reader for your whole library

Coral Reader turns your phone and tablet into a personal library. Import your own EPUB books and PDF documents, read them with a clean, paginated, fully customizable experience — then pick up right where you left off. No clutter, no noise — just comfortable, elegant reading.

EPUB & PDF

Read both EPUB books and PDF documents in one place.

📂

Library Management

Import, organize, and browse your collection in grid or list view with rich cover art.

Paginated Reader

Smooth, horizontal page-by-page reading that feels like a real book.

🎨

Reading Customization

Fonts, font size, margins, line height, spacing, alignment, brightness, and color themes.

✍️

Highlights & Notes

Mark up passages and remember what matters.

📊

Progress Tracking

Per-book reading position with an interactive progress bar and percentage.

🌙

Light & Dark Themes

Read comfortably day or night, or follow your system theme.

🌍

Localization

Full English and Hebrew support, including right-to-left (RTL) layout.
